About

Cindy-Lou Who was born and raised in Whoville. Motivated by her quest for sweets, the Christmas spirit, the fight for justice, Cindy-Lou is a powerful presence in her community. Friends describe her as independent, funny, and adorable. She is always willing to ask the tough questions. Cindy-Lou is supported by her many siblings and close community.

Experience

Cindy's List

Founder and CEO Age 3 to present
After seeing the injustice happening across Whoville, Cindy-Lou was inspired to act. She started Cindy's list, a group fighting for the rights of women who love Christmas everywhere. The team has grown to 15 members with the average age of 4. They have a strong playground presence, give out candy canes all year long, and are well known as the group to call out injustice when they see it.

Office of Mayor Augustus Maywho

Member of Tree Protection CommitteeAge 1 to 3
Cindy-Lou Who was a vital member of Mayor Maywho's Tree Protection committee. Her strong commitment to Christmas Trees made her an incredibly effective and important member. During her time on the committee, she rescued over 75 trees from chimneys across Whoville.

Education

Whoville Day Care

Age 1 to 2
When Cindy wasn't at the Mayor's office, she was learning foundational lessons at Whoville Day Care. Here, she met her team of activists, started a book club, and loved playing with blocks. Her teachers called her "kind, determined, and a team player"

Town Center Pre-School

Age 3 to present
At age 3, Cindy graduated to Town Center Pre-School. She was young for her class but a natural leader and fit in well with the other kids. At Town Center, Cindy started a club, focused on women's empowerment in other countries. She excelled in the core curriculum and often tutored other students who were struggling in class.
