Advice for the Playground

April 14, 2013

Everyone knows that the playground is the best time of the day. What is not to like? It is an amazing combination of your favorite people doing your favorite things. Many grown up people say that it is the interaction that happens on the playground when you are as young as I am that sets traditional gender roles for life. So here are a few ideas for women to help mix those roles up on a playground near you:

  1. Learn how to do the monkey bars first in your class.
  2. Run the fastest.
  3. Brag about what your mom packed you for lunch.
  4. Be really mean to everyone you like.
  5. Don't listen to the teacher's whistle.
