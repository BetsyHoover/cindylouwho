Welcome to my site!

Thanks for coming! Its great to see you. There is a conversation happening in the women's movement today that says women simply can't have it all. It is too difficult to ballance a career and a family and everything else life throws at us. So women today have to choose.

I am here to tell you that is not the case. If women make te choices they believe are right for them and their family, they can find a way to be truly happy and satisfied while constantly reaching for more.

My goal is to inspire other women like me to take a stand against this attitude. We accept the challenge at hand and seek to constantly be better. I hope you will join me in this fight! If you want to learn more, check out a little bit more about me or read an entry from my journal.
